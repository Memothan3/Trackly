🎯 THE 4 STEPS

Step 1: EMAIL ADDRESS
   • Enter your email
   • Optional: Link Google account (if Gmail)
   • Click "Next"

Step 2: PASSWORD
   • Create password (min 6 chars)
   • Confirm password
   • Click "Next"

Step 3: PROFILE DETAILS
   • Full name (required)
   • Username (required, checked for availability)
   • Phone, country, currency (optional)
   • Click "Create Account"

Step 4: EMAIL CONFIRMATION
   • Check your email
   • Click verification link
   • Sign in to dashboard

🔗 GOOGLE ACCOUNT MERGING

What it does:
   Links your Google account with your Trackly account for easy sign-in

How it works:
   1. Enter email in Step 1
   2. If Gmail, see "Link with Google Account" option
   3. Click to open Google Sign-In popup
   4. System checks if Google email matches entered email
   5. If match: ✅ Linked! Can use Google Sign-In
   6. If no match: ❌ Error shown, can retry

Email Validation:
   • Entered: john@gmail.com
   • Google: john@gmail.com
   • Result: ✅ SUCCESS - Account linked!

   • Entered: john@gmail.com
   • Google: jane@gmail.com
   • Result: ❌ ERROR - Emails don't match, try again

🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING

Wizard not showing?
   → Check browser console for errors
   → Verify JavaScript loaded
   → Refresh page (Ctrl+F5)

Google merge not working?
   → Check popup blocker settings
   → Verify Firebase OAuth configured
   → Try different browser

Username validation slow?
   → Check internet connection
   → Verify Supabase is running
   → Check browser console

Email not sending?
   → Check spam folder
   → Verify Firebase email templates
   → Check action URL configuration
